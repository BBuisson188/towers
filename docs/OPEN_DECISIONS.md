# Open Decisions for Later

These questions should not block the first vertical slice.

- Final title of the game.
- Maximum tower ownership and maximum towers deployed in one match.
- Whether each purchased gun is a permanent global unlock or an inventory item with limited copies.
- Whether currency is called coins, points, rings, or uses two currencies.
- Exact boss roster and boss attack patterns.
- Exact cancellation interactions between Void Wyrm and Blue Giant specials.
- Whether special creatures are permanently unlocked, consumed, or use cooldowns.
- Whether a shield blocks future boss/special one-hit attacks completely or only mitigates them.
- Final PvP networking model.
- Final art pipeline and 3D modeling tools.
- Monetization, if any. Do not add monetization assumptions without explicit direction.
